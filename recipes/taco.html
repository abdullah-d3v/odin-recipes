Recipe for Tacos!

Description

Learn to make tacos in the easiest way possibe by follwing the 4 steps below! (aka "Easiest way to impress your Latina Girlfrined!")

4 main types of ingredinets that are required for making tacos.

  • Meat
  • Cheese
  • Dressings
  • Veggies

4 main steps for making tacos.

  1. Protein Chicken, pork, beef, or shrimp. Whatever protein you choose, a taco surely wouldn’t be the same without the meat.
  2. Cheese Adding the cheese can be crucial to a good taco. In order to create the ultimate taco, consider putting cheese on the bottom of the shell to prevent the shell from getting soggy and topping your protein with cheese.
  3. Toppings Add your toppings. Do you like sour cream? How about guacamole? Beans? Whatever your favorite taco toppings are, this is the time to throw them on. Top off your taco with your favorite toppings to make it all yours.
  4. Veggies Don’t forget the veggies. Add the salsas and veggies last. Pico de gallo and diced tomatoes can make the taco shell soggy, and putting the lettuce on top prevents it from getting warm.
